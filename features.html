Features

osu! is a music rhythm game that has four modes of gameplay.

    • osu!: The main mode of the game. It involves hitting circles to the beat of the song.
      osu!mania: A mode where you hit keys on your keyboard to match the beat of the song.
      osu!catch: A mode where you control a basket that goes left and right in a screen to catch fruit to the beat of the song
      osu!taiko: A mode where you hit four keys to mimic drums in a song.
